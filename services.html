Services

RedCross Hospital offers a wide range of services in Medicine, Surgery, Obstetrics, Gynecology, Pediatrics, and more. We provide emergency, diagnostic, and specialized care with modern facilities.

Our hospital has fully equipped ICUs, operating rooms, consulting clinics, and a 24/7 emergency department to cater to patient needs.
